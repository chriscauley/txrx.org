As a further way to help Houstonians we will be holding workshops at TXRX Labs to help people understand the work that will need to be done to their homes to repair and also the costs associated. This will be a free service by Ronnie Devries and TXRX as a way to help the community rebuild.

TXRX's Shop Manager Ronnie Devries is a certified jack of all trades master of all from his years of work prior to TXRX but also through his hard fought years building TXRX from the ground up.

{% thumbnail "https://txrxlabs.org/static/crafters/ronnie.jpg" "600x600" as im %} {% endthumbnail %}
These workshops will cover:
  • Choosing a contractor - Discover how to find and compare contractors and services.
  • Vetting a contractor - Get questions to ask a contractor in order to prtect yourself.
  • Contracts and Payment - Navigating contracts is diffcult. Make sure you know as much as possible about payment, clauses, liability, and more.
  • Red Flags - Find out what to look for to spot deals that may be too good to be true.
